Trias stocksii Benth

Stocks Trias is an orchid named in the honor of John Ellerton Stocks. Pseudobulb are spherical, 1.5 x 1.5 cm, distantly placed on stout rhizome. Leaves are solitary, 2.5-3.5 x 1.5 cm, elliptic, pointed with 5 mm long stalk. Flowers are pinkish, 2 or 3 from the base of the pseudobulb

Botanical name: Trias stocksii
Family: Orchidaceae
Habitat: Grows on tree trunks
IUCN Status: Endangered

Ixora coccinea

Ixora coccinea, commonly known as the flame of the woods or jungle geranium, is a flowering shrub native to Southern India, Bangladesh, and Sri Lanka. It is a popular ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical regions around the world, admired for its vibrant red flowers and lush green foliage.

Scientific Name:Ixora Coccinea
Common Name:flame of the woods or jungle geranium
Habitat: Southern India, Bangladesh, and Sri Lanka.

Brahmi

Brahmi, also known as Bacopa Monnieri,is a powerful medicinal herb that has been used in Ayurvedic medicine for centuries. This plant is known for its memory enhancing properties for a very long time in India medicine. Brahmi tea that is made by brewing Brahmi leaves helps to manage cold, chest congestion and bronchitis by clearing out the mucus from the air passages which helps ease breathing. It also reduces pain and inflammation in the throat and respiratory tracts due to its anti-inflammatory property.

Scientific Name: Bacopa monnieri
Habitat: Botanical Garden, mangrove forests
IUCN Status: Endangered
